Our Mission
Inspire a profound connection with nature among all
Nature Counter empowers global communities to embrace nature, nurture well-being, and champion sustainability. Through innovative tools and guidance, we inspire profound connections, personal growth, and positive change, fostering harmony with the planet.
Our Vision
World in Harmony
We see a future where people are profoundly connected to the natural world, finding profound health, growth, and sustainability. The harmony we seek drives us to create a community deeply rooted in nature, where each individual's well-being is intertwined with our planet's health. Join us in this transformative journey toward a greener, healthier, and more sustainable Earth.
Our Objective
From the Bottom Up
We seek to eliminate the disconnect between the importance of nature and how nature is treated. Our team of researchers and experienced practitioners believe that this disconnect is so ingrained in societies that it can only change organically, from the bottom up. We believe when more individuals are aware of the importance of nature, they will be more likely to embrace the preservation of nature as a goalâ€”one which the global climate change crisis makes all the more important.
Our Values
Enriching Natural Relationships
We prize deep connections with nature, enriching lives and fostering profound bonds. Beyond mere appreciation, these connections are essential elements nurturing the human spirit, making every experience a gateway to lasting relationships.
Igniting Outdoor Passions
Dedicated to igniting exploration, we breathe life into every expedition. Shared stories awaken wanderlust, shaping adventures, and kindling love for nature, transforming ordinary moments into lifelong journeys.
Wellness Harmony
Rooted in our ethos is enhancing physical and mental health. Nature's therapeutic canvas offers joy-filled days, invigorating and healing, bringing boundless happiness. It's the profound rejuvenation uniquely provided by nature.
Support a Sustainable Planet
Championing eco-conscious living as our core responsibility. Our approach defines sustainable choices, ensuring a vibrant Earth. Sustainability isn't a buzzword; it's our endorsed way of life, embodying our mission's heart.
