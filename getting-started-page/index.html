Getting Started
Welcome to Nature Counter, your gateway to a deeper connection with nature! Signing up with Nature Counter is the first step toward enriching your outdoor experiences. With just a few clicks, you can set up your account and unlock a world of nature exploration. Once your account is ready, dive into the app and start your outdoor journey.
Signing Up
Signing up with Nature Counter is easy! Simply download the app, complete the onboarding process, tap on ‘Sign Up’, and follow the prompts to enter your details. Once completed, you're ready to embark on your outdoor adventures.
Setting Up Your Account
To access your account, tap on your profile icon located at the top right corner of the Home screen. In the ‘My Account’ section, you can manage your profile information, update your preferences, and customize your experience.
