Finding Articles
Explore curated topics like focus, immunity, memory, and sleep. Tailor your reading experience to your wellness interests effortlessly. Enjoy a seamless journey through nature’s wisdom.
Filtering by Category
Navigate to the 'Articles' page by tapping the 'Articles icon' in the bottom navigation menu. Next, click the 'Category' dropdown to select your preferred topic, filtering the articles accordingly.
Reading an Article
Tap on an article to read it. You can scroll up and down to read the entire article. To exit the 'Article Reader' page, tap the Back Button to return to the 'Articles' page.
